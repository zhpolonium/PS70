### About Me Hello there! You want to learn a little bit about me, I see. Well, Winter Zhang is my name. I'm a first-year graduate student from Brown University. I majored in NanoEngineering and Product Design for my undergraduate study and Innovation Management and Entrepreneurship for graduate study.
